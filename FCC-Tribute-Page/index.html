Bruce Lee

Bruce Lee, A philospher, martial artist and most importantly an inspiration to many.

Why I like him?

Since childhood, I was a fan of martial arts and I think I have seen almost all of the movies of Bruce Lee. I just loved his acting, mastery of skills and determination to train hard.


A little Intro: Bruce Lee (1940-1973) was a Hong Kong and American actor, film director, martial artist, martial arts instructor, philosopher,and founder of the martial art Jeet Kune Do, one of the wushu or kungfu styles.

Interesting Facts

  • Kareem Abdul Jabbar was student of Bruce Lee
  • At 16, Lee began training under Yip Man
  • He could take a coin out of your hand as you closed it
  • He only ever lost one fight.
  • He decided to start his own style of martial arts after not beating an opponent quickly enough.
